Tutorial

Goal

Win 3 small boards in a row (like classic Tic Tac Toe) on a big 3x3 grid of boards. Or win 5 small boards.

How To Play

1. The game has 9 small Tic Tac Toe boards arranged in a 3x3 grid.

2. Player X starts on any cell in any board.

3. The cell you play in decides which small board your opponent must play in next.

4. If the required board is full or won, your opponent can play anywhere.
